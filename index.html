Bizard Tunnel
A service that introduces the long-awaited transparency and openness to the working processes of mining parks and data centers. With this application, equipment holders will be able to receive information about the devices operation, make decisions quickly and efficiently and maintain a dialogue with service providers.
Handlet
This unique technology, which is unrivaled throughout the world, will allow to secure digital assets to the greatest possible extent. To identify user the system: — uses data obtained as a result of scanning the hand; — transforms it into a part of a private key and makes it impossible to access the wallet by third parties; — excludes the possibility of loss or theft of the wallet.
